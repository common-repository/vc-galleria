== Description ==

It's a simple and elegant slider that works awesome on mobile,it has been made with "mobile first" in mind.
It's visual composer extension that just works,and works well on mobile(no lag, no freeze, no nothin).
I have made this extension because i could not find one that worked well on my mobile.

== Installation ==

Navigate to Plugins->Add new from your wordpress admin,after it's uploaded just click activate and that's it.
